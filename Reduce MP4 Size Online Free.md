## Why MP4 Files Get Large

MP4 files often become too large because of:

- high bitrate
- 1080p or 4K resolution
- long duration
- large audio tracks
- exports made with editing software defaults

## Best Way to Reduce MP4 Size

For most users, the best workflow is:

1. upload the MP4 file
2. choose balanced compression
3. keep MP4 output for compatibility
4. lower resolution only if needed

## When to Lower Resolution

If your file is still too large after normal compression, reduce:

- 4K to 1080p
- 1080p to 720p
- 720p to 480p

Lowering bitrate alone is often enough for shorter MP4 files, but downscaling helps more when the source is oversized.
