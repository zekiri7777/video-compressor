H.264 is usually better for compatibility, while H.265 is usually better for achieving a smaller file size at similar visual quality.

## H.264 Benefits

- broad compatibility
- fast playback support
- good for websites, email, and messaging apps

## H.265 Benefits

- stronger compression efficiency
- smaller files at similar quality
- useful when storage or upload speed matters more

## Which One Should You Choose?

Choose H.264 if:

- you want the safest playback compatibility
- you are sharing across many devices
- you need predictable results

Choose H.265 if:

- the source file is very large
- smaller output matters more than universal support
- your users or devices support HEVC playback
