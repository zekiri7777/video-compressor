Email systems usually have strict attachment limits, so the fastest solution is to compress the file before sending.

## Why Email Video Attachments Fail

Large videos exceed attachment limits because of:

- high resolution
- long duration
- excessive bitrate
- audio that is larger than necessary

## Best Compression Strategy for Email

For most email use cases:

- reduce to MP4
- use balanced or strong compression
- lower resolution to 720p if needed
- keep duration short when possible

## When Compression Is Better Than Cloud Sharing

Compression is useful when:

- the recipient needs a direct attachment
- you want faster send and download times
- the video is short enough to remain usable after compression
